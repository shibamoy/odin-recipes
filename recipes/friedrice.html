Mastering the Wok

To be the best at cooking, one must master the use of the wok. One must simply just do it

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of leftover rice
  • 1 bag of assorted veggies
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp butter

Do not put too much effort into cooking, just throw all of the ingredients together and it will be good.

The Process...

  1. Heat up the wok on high heat
  2. Throw the veggies in with some butter and soy sauce
  3. Add the rice and stir everything together. Remember to flick wrist
  4. Lastly, add butter and oyster sauce
  5. Enjoy the fried rice!
