Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ribeye steak
  • 2 tbsp of salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp of its not butter
  • a pinch of snow

How to cook the steak

  1. Let your meat sit out for about an hour
  2. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top of the steak
  3. Heat the pan on medium high, then throw the steak on
  4. 4 minutes on each side for a nice medium cook and add butter to the pan on low heat
  5. Take steak out and let it sit for 8 minutes then enjoy!
