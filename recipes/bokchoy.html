Veggies for Vegans a.k.a BOKCHOY

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of bokchoy
  • 3 tbsp of sugar
  • oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp of minced garlic

Very simple dish to cook in under 6 minutes and in 4 steps!

How to become a vegan

  1. On medium heat, add bokchoy in with a bit of water and stir for 2 minutes
  2. Drizzle oyster sauce and add the minced garlic
  3. Cover lid for 2 minutes and let it steam for a bit
  4. Add sugar on top and serve dish while it is nice a hot!
